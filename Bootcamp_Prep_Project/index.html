Scuderia Ferrari Formula 1 Team

Significant Moments in the Team's History

1929-1950
Ferrari team was founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, initially to race cars produced by Alfa Romeo.
By 1947, Ferrari had begun building its own cars.
In October 1939, Enzo Ferrari founded Auto Avio Costruzioni Ferrari, which also manufactured machine tools.
1950
Ferrari debuted in Formula 1 at the Monaco Grand Prix.
1952
The first Formula 1 Championship is won with Alberto Ascari as Ferrari driver: 7 wins in 7 races for the Scuderia.
Next year, in 1953, the title was his again.
until 2000
Ferrari drivers gained 7 World Champion titles, the last of them was in 1979.
2000-2004
Michael Schumacher's era:
he came to the team in 1996 and took 3d place in his 1st year for Ferrari.
In 2000 he eventually won the title in drivers' competition and maintained this title for 4 years more.
He left the team in 2006.
2007
Kimi Räikkönen won the title for Ferrari.
until 2020
There were many outstanding drivers in Ferrari cars, like Fernando Alonso and Sebastian Vettel. But they didn't bring new titles for Scuderia.
2020
Ferrari faced a challenging season, finishing 6th in the Constructors' Championship.
It was the team's worst performance in decades.
2021-2024
Ferrari began to improve with a more competitive car, driven by Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz.
2025
Lewis Hamilton (a seven-time Formula One World Drivers' Champion) will replace Carlos Sainz.

All-Time Favourite Ferrari Drivers

Alberto Ascari

  • Years with Ferrari: 1950-1953
  • 2 Drivers' Championship Titles

Niki Lauda

  • Years with Ferrari: 1974-1977
  • 2 Drivers' Championships Titles
  • In 1976 his car caught on fire during a crash, he was seriously injured, but returned to raising in the same year

Gilles Villeneuve

  • Years with Ferrari: 1977-1982
  • Never won a World Championship
  • But his passionate and fearless driving made him one of Ferrari's most beloved drivers
  • His tragic death in 1982 solidified his legendary status

Michael Schumacher

  • Years with Ferrari: 1996-2006
  • 5 Drivers' Championships
  • Schumacher is arguably the greatest driver in Ferrari history

Kimi Räikkönen

  • Years with Ferrari: 2007-2009, 2014-2018
  • 1 Drivers' Championship
  • Kimi is known for his laid-back attitude and his cool, consistent driving

Fernando Alonso

  • Years with Ferrari: 2010-2014
  • Never won a title with Ferrari
  • But his skill and determination made him one of the team's most respected drivers

Sebastian Vettel

  • Years with Ferrari: 2015-2020
  • He is another driver who came close to winning a championship

Charles Leclerc

  • Years with Ferrari: 2019-present
  • He secured several wins and podiums with Ferrari
  • He's seen as the future of Ferrari

Carlos Sainz

  • Years with Ferrari: 2021-2024
  • He is seen as a rising star within the Ferrari team

Lewis Hamilton

  • Years with Ferrari: the first season with Ferrari in 2025
  • He is a 7-time Formula 1 World Drivers' Champion

More Facts About Scuderia Ferrari Team

Since 1943 both Scuderia Ferrari and Ferrari's road car factory have based at Maranello
The team owns and operates a test track on the same site, the Fiorano Circuit, built in 1972, which is used for testing road and race cars
The team is named after its founder Enzo Ferrari.
Scuderia means 'stable' in the Italian Language
The prancing horse was the symbol used on Italian World War I ace Francesco Baracca's fighter plane. It became the logo of Ferrari after the fallen ace's parents, close acquaintances of Enzo Ferrari, suggested that Ferrari use the symbol for good luck
In keeping with Italian roots, the Ferrari team has almost always kept a red colour 'rosso corsa', the national racing colour of Italy
Frederic Vasseur, Team Principal since 2023
The Italian Grand Prix at Monza is regarded as the team's home race.
Fans of Ferrari are known as 'tifosi' and are said to be some of the most passionate in the sport.
